About C Division League
The C Division League, run by the Tripura Football Association (TFA), is the starting level of football in Tripura. It gives new and young players a chance to show their talent and improve. The league is held every year, and teams compete to win and move up to higher divisions. It also helps grow interest in football and brings local communities together.
